User Guide

Viewing the Schedule
▶

The Schedule tab shows this week's volunteer shifts.

Use ← Prev and Next → to browse other weeks, or tap Today to jump back.

On phones, days appear as an accordion (tap to expand). On larger screens, you'll see a weekly grid.

Chip colors:

Green = Here Red = Away Yellow = Late Dashed = Needs sub Blue = Substitute
Marking Attendance
▶

Tap any volunteer's name chip to open the attendance editor.

Choose a status:

  • Here — default, attending as normal
  • Away — not coming (a dashed "needs sub" indicator appears)
  • Late — arriving late
  • Partial — only part of the shift (add times in notes)

Add optional notes (e.g. "leaving by 12:45") and tap Save.

No login is needed to mark attendance.

Substituting for Someone
▶

When a volunteer is marked Away, a + Sub button appears next to their name.

Tap + Sub, pick the volunteer who is filling in, and save. The substitute appears as a blue chip next to the away volunteer.

To remove a sub, an admin can delete the attendance record.

My Shifts
▶

The My Shifts tab lets you manage your shift preferences.

Select your name from the dropdown, then check or uncheck the shifts you'd like to be scheduled for (Mon-Fri, Morning/Afternoon/Evening).

Below the preferences grid you'll see your assigned shifts for the current week with their attendance status and who else is on each shift.

You can also add your email address for shift notifications. A verification link will be sent to confirm your address.

Copy & Calendar
▶

Copy Schedule: Tap the Copy button next to "Today" on the Schedule tab. The week's schedule is copied as plain text you can paste into a message or email.

Calendar Feed: Subscribe to the shift calendar from your phone's calendar app:

  1. Copy this URL: https://mngcuykpdpvunenujdsa.supabase.co/functions/v1/calendar-feed
  2. In Apple Calendar: File → New Calendar Subscription → paste the URL
  3. In Google Calendar: Other calendars + → From URL → paste

The calendar auto-updates with shift changes. For your shifts only, add ?volunteer_id=YOUR_ID to the URL.

Offline Mode
▶

The app works offline! If you lose your internet connection, a yellow bar appears at the top.

You can still mark attendance while offline — your changes are saved locally and automatically synced when you reconnect.

Admin Features
▶

The Admin tab requires a magic link login (enter your email, check inbox, click the link).

Once signed in you can:

  • View weekly attendance stats (expected, attending, away, subs, unfilled)
  • View 4-week attendance trends
  • Browse different weeks with the week navigation
  • Add new volunteers or edit existing ones (name, email, phone, active/inactive)
  • Manage shift assignments (click "Shifts" next to a volunteer's name)
